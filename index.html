About

Hi! I am Safae Nassif, I am a computer science engineer and a passionate artist and musician.My native language is French and Arabic, and I also speak English and Japanese fluently.I studied music and guitar at the Conservatory of Music for eight years, developing my skills and deepening my love for music. Since 2008, I have actively participated in a wide range of events, including music festivals, competitions, and performances across Morocco, where I've had the honor of sharing the stage with renowned Moroccan artists.

My journey has also taken me to radio platforms and community events, where I've brought music to diverse audiences. Beyond live performances, I share my artistry on my YouTube channel, where I upload covers of songs in Arabic, French, English, and Japanese, showcasing my versatility and connection to global music.
